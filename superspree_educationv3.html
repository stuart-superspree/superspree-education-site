Superspree Education — Strategic Positioning

Superspree began as a technology company making eco-designed hardware. A global development research programme transformed it into something more ambitious: a behavioural science-led membership ecosystem that happens to deploy elegant devices.

The Loop requires no power, no WiFi, no app and no login. One tap from any smartphone. But the device is not the product. The research, the training and the membership are the product — and every future development integrates into what you already have. No new platforms. No retraining. No rip-and-replace.
